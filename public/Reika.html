Mod Description
Sea to Sea (abbreviated C2C) is a very large progression and exploration expansion mod for Subnautica, designed to dramatically expand and extend the gameplay of a typical playthrough, both in terms of duration and amount of used content. This is done by making many more regions of the map and game mechanics necessary for the core progression (both story completion and escape in the Neptune rocket), primarily by adding location-unique resources, blueprints, items, or knowledge, and situational needs for those resources, tools, or knowledge. Sea to Sea aims to preserve the original gameplay as much as possible, building upon it rather than replacing it. As such, a C2C playthrough should still have strong thematic similarities, and a nearly-identical narrative experience, to that of the vanilla game.
